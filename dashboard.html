How the numbers work

Each row is one ad/variant/day. CPC=spend÷clicks, CAC=spend÷new fans, LTV=revenue÷new fans, ROAS=revenue÷spend, Profit=revenue−spend. The call is SCALE when ROAS ≥ the scale threshold, CUT when ROAS < the cut threshold, otherwise KEEP. Net profit = ad profit − the creator's pay (salary or %).
